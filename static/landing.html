Why PaperCut?

Transcript Editing

Your video becomes a text document. Delete the sentences you don't want — no timeline, no learning curve.

Silence Detection

Automatically finds and removes dead air, ums, and pauses using auto-editor under the hood.

Multi-Format Export

Get back an FCPXML, Premiere XML, DaVinci timeline, or a finished video. Your file, your NLE, no lock-in.

How it works

1

Upload your video

Drop your video file in the browser. PaperCut transcribes it locally — nothing leaves your machine.

2

Edit the transcript

Read through the transcript and delete the blocks you don't want. It's like editing a document.

3

Export your edit

PaperCut merges your cuts with silence removal and exports to whatever format your editor needs.
